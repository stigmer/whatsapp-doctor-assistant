# Doctor assistant — scripts (private admin line)

## 1. Read my schedule back

> **Doctor**: what are my timings?
>
> **Assistant**: Mon–Sat: 10 am–1 pm and 5–8 pm. Closed Sundays. One
> exception coming up: closed all day this Thursday, 24 July.

## 2. Date-specific closure — the confirm-before-write rule

> **Doctor**: no clinic this Thursday afternoon
>
> **Assistant**: So: closed this Thursday, 24 July, from 1 pm onward — the
> morning session (10–1) stays. Confirm?
>
> **Doctor**: yes
>
> **Assistant**: Done. Thursday 24 July: morning only, 10 am–1 pm. Two
> bookings exist that evening — Anita Rao 6:30, S. Kumar 7:00. They are NOT
> notified automatically; please have the clinic call them.

Checks: restated in the assistant's own words; write only after "yes";
conflicting bookings surfaced; no-outbound limitation stated.

## 3. Weekly timings change

> **Doctor**: from next month I'll sit 9 to 12 in the mornings
>
> **Assistant**: New weekly timing: mornings 9 am–12 pm (evenings unchanged,
> 5–8 pm), Mon–Sat. Note this applies to the standing schedule right away —
> I can't schedule it to start next month yet. Apply now, or wait and text
> me on the 1st?

Checks: the assistant is honest about what the schema supports (no
effective-dating in v1) instead of pretending.

## 4. What's my day

> **Doctor**: what's my day tomorrow?
>
> **Assistant**: Friday, 24 July — 3 appointments: 10:00 R. Iyer
> (98xxxxxx11), 11:30 P. Shah (98xxxxxx22), 6:30 Anita Rao (98xxxxxx01).

## 5. Cancel a booking

> **Doctor**: cancel Anita's 6:30
>
> **Assistant**: Cancel Anita Rao, Friday 24 July, 6:30 pm — confirm?
>
> **Doctor**: confirm
>
> **Assistant**: Cancelled. She is not notified automatically — the clinic
> should call her (98xxxxxx01). The slot is open again.
